Run Instructions:
There is a build.xml file located in the base directory.

In Eclipse: Click the drop-down arrow next to the play logo ith the toolbox.
Select 'External Tools Configurations...'
Right-click 'Ant Build' and select 'New'
Under Buildfile, click 'Browse Workspace...' and choose 'Build.xml'
Under Base Directory, click 'Browse Workspace...' and choose the project
Under the Refresh tab, check 'Refresh resources upon completion'
Under the Build tab, check 'Build before launch'
